Mini-Biography

Brandon Lee was born on February 1, 1965 to Bruce Lee and Linda Emery. He has one younger sister, Shannon Lee.

His education included majoring in Theater while attendingEmerson College in Boston, Massachusetts. He later moved to New York City, and took acting lessons at the Lee Strasber Theatre & Film Institute.

Brandon was an avid martial artist and actor. He was known to take his roles seriously. For example, he learned how to play the guitar for his role as Eric Draven. Draven is the protagonist of "The Crow", who was the guitarist of the fictional band, "Hangman's Joke".

Brandon was engaged to Eliza Hutton. Unfortunately, Brandon suffered an accident while filming "The Crow" and passed away on March 31, 1993.

Brandon was laid to rest on April 3, 1993, in Lakeview Cemetary (Seattle, Washingtom). He was buried beside his father.
